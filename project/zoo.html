ZOO & AQUARIUM

Previous Next

>>>>>Ragunan Zoo

Ragunan Zoo is located in Pasar Minggu, South Jakarta, Indonesia. The zoo has a land area of 140-hectare (350-acre). The zoo has an aviary and included a primate centre, and employs over 450 people. Many of the animals are endangered and threatened from all parts of Indonesia and the rest of the world. There are a total of 3,122 animal specimens including birds. Laid out in a lush tropical setting, rare animals such as crocodile, gorilla, orangutan, tapir, anoa, sumatran tiger, babirusa and peacocks are given ample room.

>>>>>Fauna Land

Faunaland is a zoo located inside Allianz Ecopark and stands on an area of approximately 5 hectares consisting of land and water. Faunaland carries the concept of Papua which is a fusion between the continents of Asia and Australia so as to create the beauty of flora, fauna and the cultural arts of the area. This concept presents unique and rare animals from various regions in Indonesia, especially animals from eastern Indonesia. A reptile exhibit is currently under construction. The zoo have an white siamang named Otong, which is the only one in the world.

>>>>>Suaka Margasatwa Muara Angke

Muara Angke Wildlife Reserve (SMMA) is the home for various species of birds and other animals which have been difficult to find in other Jakarta areas. Jakarta Green Monster recorded that there are a total of 91 bird species, namely 28 water bird species and 63 forest bird species, that live in this area. About 17 of them are protected bird species. Apart from bird species, at SMMA there are still groups of wild kra monkeys, another mammal species, and various species of reptile. SSMA is located in the mangrove forest area(mangrove) on the north coast of Jakarta.

>>>>>Taman Burung

Taman Burung is divided into five exhibits: Kubah barat (west dome) which include birds on the Sumatra, Borneo, Java, and lesser Sunda islands, kubah timur (east dome) which include birds from Sulawesi, Maluku Islands, and New Guinea, river exhibits, individual exhibits, and raptor exhibits. The park also own several birds from outside of Indonesia, such as pheasants from China and crowned crane from Africa.

>>>>>Aquarium Jakarta

The Jakarta Aquarium is a marine and freshwater aquarium located within a retail and leisure complex Neo Soho in Jakarta, Indonesia. The aquarium is home to hundreds of mammal, reptile, insect and various types of Indonesian marine fish, is to introduce to the next generation the biodiversity of the archipelago starting from islands, forests, and mangroves.

>>>>>Sea World

Seaworld Indonesia is a miniature sea charm contained in the first tourist complex at Telaga Golf and the second integrated Ancol Jakarta Baycity.

Division in Seaworld Indonesia
The main aquarium maintains thousands of Indonesian marine animals. A total of 35,500 Indonesian marine fish from 35 different species are kept here.
The Freshwater Area is equipped with collections of freshwater animals from various countries, including piranhas and Arapaima gigas from the Amazon river.
Lorong Antasena is an underwater passageway that is 80 m long operated by an automatic walkway with a see-through dome.
Aquarium Dugong is home to rare marine mammals, namely Dugong ( Dugong dugon ) and Otter's Track is home to the semi-aquatic mammal Aonyx cinerea .

>>>>>Dunia Air Tawar

Built since 1992 and inaugurated on 20 April 1994. This aquarium park is the largest and most comprehensive in the world and second largest in Asia. Of freshwater biota park Save 6000 animals of 126 species, are of various kinds, both of the various waters of Indonesia and around the world, including aquatic plants, reptiles, crustaceans, and fish. This aquarium park has a museum, library, auditorium, aquarium archipelago, reptiles corner, hallway carp, and built quarantine room at the rear to accommodate the collection and breeding of the farmers who could be traded to the visitors, the general public, reservoir fish, and exporters.

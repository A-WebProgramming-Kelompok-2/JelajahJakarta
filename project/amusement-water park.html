AMUSEMENT PARK

>>>>> Dufan ( Dunia Fantasi )

Dunia Fantasi, which opened to the public on August 29, 1986, and popularly known as Dufan , is the first theme park developed by Ancol. Dufan is the largest outdoor entertainment center in Indonesia that pampers visitors with Fantasy Around the World, through various high-tech game content, which is divided into 8 regions, namely: Indonesia, Jakarta, Asia, Europe, America, Greece, Hikayat and Balada Kera. The company also made Dufan as one of the edutainment centers in Ancol, namely by opening the Fantasy World Physics (Fidufa) and Pentas Prestasi. Dufan has been certified ISO 9001: 2008 since 2009.

>>>>> TMII (Taman Mini Indonesia Indah)

Taman Mini "Indonesia Indah" (TMII) is a culture-based recreational area located in East Jakarta, Indonesia. It is operated by Yayasan Harapan Kita, a foundation established by Siti Hartinah, the first lady during most of the New Order and wife of Suharto, and still run by Suharto's descendants since his death. It has an area of about 100 hectares (250 acres). The park is a synopsis of Indonesian culture, with virtually all aspects of daily life in Indonesia's 26 (in 1975) provinces encapsulated in separate pavilions with the collections of rumah adat as the example of Indonesian vernacular architecture, clothing, dances and traditions are all depicted impeccably.

>>>>> Kidzania

KidZania Jakarta is the first South East Asia theme park or a 'city' for children which mix the elements of education and entertainment for children ages 2-16 years old. Specially designed and built to resemble a replica of a real city, but the scale of the children, complete with highways, urban facilities such as hospitals, supermarkets, salons, theater, factories and much more. In this city children can play a role or adult profession, such as being a doctor, pilot, police, cooks, more than 100 scientists and other professions. In Indonesia, KidZania occupies a space of 7,500 m2 located on the 6th floor of Pacific Place Mall, Sudirman Central Business District, Jl. Jend. Sudirman Kav 52-53, Jakarta, Indonesia with maximum visitor capacity of 1,800 people in one shift.

WATER PARK

>>>>> Atlantis Water Adventure Ancol

Atlantis Water Adventure (AWA) is the second theme park developed by Ancol and stands on an area of ​​5 hectares. AWA is the result of the revitalization of the Ancol Swimming Pool Water Recreation Park which will provide visitors with water tourism adventures with 8 main pools, namely: Poseidon, Antila, Plaza Atlas, Aquarius, Octopus, Atlantean, and Kiddy Pool.

>>>>> WaterBom Jakarta

Waterbom Jakarta is a family recreation center that focuses on outdoor activities and water activities. Waterbom PIK has been operating since 27 October 2007 with the concept of returning to nature, equipped with tall tropical trees, green and lush shrubs with a beautiful garden setting. Combined with modern and minimalist building architecture, Waterbom PIK takes the theme Urban Cool .
